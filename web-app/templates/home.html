How To Download Your YouTube Watch History:

  1. Go to Google Takeout.
  2. Deselect all options, then scroll down and select YouTube and YouTube Music.
  3. Click on the button that says Multiple Formats, scroll down to History, and select JSON.
  4. Click Next step, then Create export.
  5. Once the export is ready, download the zip file, extract it, and upload it here!
